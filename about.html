About NeatDrops

Delivering excellence through innovation, reliability, and customer-focused logistics solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, NeatDrops Logistics began with a simple mission: to transform the delivery experience for businesses across East Africa. Our founder, Joram Mwesigwa, recognized the logistical challenges that businesses faced and set out to create a solution that combined efficiency, reliability, and cutting-edge technology.

What started as a small operation with just three vehicles has grown into a comprehensive logistics network serving hundreds of businesses. Our success stems from our unwavering commitment to understanding each client's unique needs and developing tailored solutions that address their specific challenges.

Today, NeatDrops is recognized as a leader in the logistics industry, known for our innovative approach, exceptional customer service, and consistent delivery performance. We continue to expand our services while maintaining the personalized attention that has been the foundation of our growth.
